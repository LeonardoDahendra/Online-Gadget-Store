OUR STORY
STORY
GoLitech's journey began in 2000, when they first established themselves as a small company in Jakarta, Indonesia. Over the years, their commitment to delivering high-quality computer peripherals has earned them a strong reputation among consumers in the country. With their relentless pursuit of excellence and customer satisfaction, GoLitech gradually expanded their presence, reaching every corner of Indonesia. Now, with their sights set on the global market, GoLitech is preparing to take their brand beyond borders and share their top-notch computer peripherals with customers worldwide. Their unwavering dedication to quality, innovation, and expanding accessibility to technology makes GoLitech a trusted name in the industry, set to make a lasting impact on the international stage.
HISTORY
2000-2004
GoLitech, founded in 2000, encountered various challenges at the start. Limited financial resources and stiff competition posed significant hurdles. However, GoLitech strategically addressed these obstacles by establishing strong partnerships, optimizing resources, prioritizing innovation, and implementing effective marketing strategies.
2005-2010
As GoLitech gained popularity and success, the company recognized the opportunity to expand its business. GoLitech began opening new stores in various cities and regions across the country, with each new store GoLitech are able to reach a wider customer base and cater to the growing demand for their products.
2011-Now
Through unwavering dedication, Golitech have established themselves as a prominent player in the industry. With a vision for global expansion, GoLitech is committed to bringing their innovative products to customers worldwide. GoLitech aims to establish a strong global presence and solidify their position as a leading provider of cutting-edge technology.
